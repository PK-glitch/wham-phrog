Battlefield One

Battlefield one is a world war one based shooter.
was produced by EA and Dice and released on the
21st October 2016, it received a positive reaction
at launch with its trailer.

The Battlefield franchise has a history of complex easter eggs in their games. in Battlefield 1, they took it to another level, with the Peacekeeper easter egg.

This is a guide for how to get the Peacekeeper

There are 7 things you need to have to get the Peacekeeper, these are:

  1. The 'A Beginning' dogtag
  2. The 'A Conflict' dogtag
  3. The 'An Omen' dogtag
  4. The 'Angel sighting' back dogtag
  5. The 'Belly of The Beast' back dogtag
  6. The 'An Escalation' skin for the m1918 MG

    7. Once you have done all of these easter eggs, you will

    have to go into the map Paschendaele and do a final easter egg

    to finally unlock the Peacekeeper revolver.

    What you unlock is the most powerful revolver, with some

    incredible, smoothe animations which make you feel like you've

    achieved something great that not very many people have.
